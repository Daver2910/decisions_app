Introduction

Decision-making is a complex process of defining and choosing based on the values and preferences of the decision-maker. This tool will help you to verbalise the alternatives, clarify your priorities and make a final choice between two or more options. Each criteria influencing your choice will be valuated by you which makes the result reflect your personal beliefs and preferences. You will see that all the decisions we make, even emotional ones only seems irrational, when in the reality they are composed of several rational components impacting your choice.

Asking the Question

Please ask the question containing two or more options. The tool is not suppose to reply yes or no questions. For example: a.) Should I move to USA or stay in Poland? b.) Should I propose to Veronica or wait? 3.) Should I buy a car or use public transport?
